---
title: "Community Celebrates New Mt Albert Mural"
date: 2024-11-15
summary: "Over 100 community members gathered for the unveiling of the new community mural in Mt Albert."
---

Last weekend saw the community come together to celebrate the unveiling of our newest public art project - a vibrant mural created collaboratively by local artists and residents.

The project, which took three months to complete, involved over 50 community members in the design and painting process. The mural celebrates the diverse stories and cultures that make up our neighbourhood.

"This has been an incredible journey," said lead artist Maria Santos. "Seeing the community come together to create something beautiful has been the highlight of my year."

The mural is located on the wall of the Mt Albert Community Centre and is now a permanent part of our neighbourhood's landscape.
