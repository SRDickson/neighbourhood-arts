Our youth workshop series provides free creative opportunities for young people in Albert-Eden.

## Program Overview

Each term, we offer a series of workshops led by professional artists, introducing participants to different creative mediums including:

- Printmaking
- Sculpture
- Digital art
- Mixed media

## Goals

- Provide accessible creative opportunities for local youth
- Connect young people with professional artists
- Build confidence and creative skills
- Create a supportive community of young creatives
