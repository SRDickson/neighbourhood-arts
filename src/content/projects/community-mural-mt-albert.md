This vibrant community mural project brought together local artists and residents to create a lasting piece of public art in Mt Albert.

## The Project

Working with local artist collective and community volunteers, we transformed a blank wall into a celebration of our neighbourhood's diverse stories and cultures.

## Outcomes

- Over 50 community members participated in painting days
- Local school children contributed design ideas
- The mural has become a landmark and gathering point for the community

## Partners

We worked with Mt Albert Community Centre and local businesses to make this project possible.
