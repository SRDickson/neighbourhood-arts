Sandringham Sounds brought live music back to our local parks with a series of free, family-friendly outdoor concerts.

## About the Series

Over four weekends, we hosted local musicians in different parks around Sandringham, creating opportunities for neighbours to gather, enjoy music, and connect with each other.

## Featured Artists

The series showcased a diverse range of local talent, from acoustic singer-songwriters to multicultural performance groups.

## Impact

- 4 concerts held across different parks
- Over 800 attendees across the series
- 12 local musicians and groups performed
